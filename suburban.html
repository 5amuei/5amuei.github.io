Overview

This section of my portfolio covers my restoration and modification of a 1999 Suburban using parts from a totalled 1999 Tahoe. The Suburban has recieved a new engine, drivetrain, suspension, brakes, and interior. Modifications performed have been almost entirely designed, coded, and built by me. So far the modifications have been an android app to remote start system When I began my graduate degree I had been gifted a totalled 1999 Chevy Tahoe from my parents as a graduation present because I needed a car. The Tahoe had been well cared for and if it was not for the accident would have been in pristine condition.
